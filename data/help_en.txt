**MAKLUMAT**
`+info (member)` - Get information of a member.
`+server` - Get information about the server.
`+about` - Get information about Polar Bot.

**UTILITY**
`+nick [new name]` - Change your display name.
`+clearnick` - Reset your display name.
`+yt [query]` - Search for YouTube links.
`+kamus [word]` - Search for a word in Bahasa Melayu.
`+choose [choice 1] [choice 2] ...` - Randomly choose from a list of words.
`+roll [num]` - Obtain a random number between 1 and a desired number.
`+random [num]` - Obtain a random number between a desired number and another.
`+translate [text] (output language) (input language)` - Translate a text.
`+give [role]` - Give yourself a role.
`+colour [colour]` - Change the colour of your name.

**RECREATIONAL**
`+me [text]` - An adoption of Skype's '/me' command.
`+ping` - Pong!
`+reverse [text]` - Reverse a text.
`+mirror [text]` - Mirror a text.
`+invert [text]` - Flip a text vertically.
`+google [query]` - Return a google link.
`+lmgtfy [query]` - Create a LMGTFY link.
`+convert [value] (base) (rate)` - Convert currency. Rates are updated daily at 11pm.

Type `+help tag` to find out more about the tag command.
